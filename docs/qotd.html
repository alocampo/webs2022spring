Questions of The Day

In August 2015, at the age of 12, I bought a book called "Q&A a Day: 5-Year Journal" with the intentions of writing my answer every day for the next five years. It's been seven years now, and with the amount of blank lines, the absolute earliest I could possibly fill in the whole book is May 2025. That's a whole decade later! I'm desperately trying to be nice to my younger self but truly a lot of this book is kind of cringey. Still, I can't help but be fond of how my life slowly changed over these questions. Below is a selection of some of the questions that most depict who I've become over time.
